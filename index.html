About Us




We are a team of students who want to raise awareness about cyber security. We believe that cyber security is not only important for businesses and governments, but also for individuals and communities. Our project aims to raise awareness and educate people about the risks and challenges of cyber security, as well as the best practices and tools to protect themselves online. We hope that our project will inspire others to learn more about cyber security and to take action to safeguard their digital lives.
